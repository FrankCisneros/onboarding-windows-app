👋 About Me
## 🔍 Research & Learn

* **What is a “User Manual for Me”, and why do teams use them?**

It's a personal document where I explain how I work, how you communicate, and what you need to optimize your work.

* **What categories are helpful to include? (e.g., communication style, work habits, feedback preferences)**

  * **About Me** (Who I am)
  * **Communication Style** (How I prefer to communicate)
  * **Work Habits** (How I work best)
  * **Feedback Preferences** (How I give and receive feedback)
  * **Tools and Preferences** (Technologies or ways of working)

* **How can writing a personal user manual improve teamwork and reduce misunderstandings?**

  * Avoid thinking someone is ignoring me when they are actually following their usual work method
  * Improve the collaboration and adaptation process when working in a team

## 📝 Reflection
* **What do you need from teammates to do your best work? (e.g., direct communication, flexibility, detailed instructions, minimal interruptions)**

  * Clear requirements; they don't need to be perfect explanations, but they should be as understandable as possible.
  * Time to analyze the technical situations I'm working on.

* **How do you prefer to communicate? (e.g., async messages, video calls, quick check-ins, long discussions)**

  * Direct messages via any chat platform (I don't usually check Gmail much)
  * Calls to explain a problem more directly or something I need to understand better
  * I prefer clarity and for people to be completely direct

* **What helps you stay focused and productive? (e.g., quiet workspace, deep work sessions, structured to-do lists, music)**

  * Work with music I like and without external noise
  * Understand the tasks I have to perform
  * Keep my desk and work environment, both internal and external (software), tidy

* **How do you like to give and receive feedback? (e.g., clear & direct, with positive reinforcement, written vs. verbal feedback)**

  * Crticias constructivas que me ayuden a mejorar
  * Que sea directo y claro

* **What are some quirks or things to know about your work style? (e.g., need time to process ideas, like to brainstorm out loud, prefer written instructions)**

  * I need a certain amount of time to reply to messages (less than 10 minutes maximum).
  * If I'm quiet for a long time with my headphones on, it's because I'm concentrating on something.
  * I often critique how apps are made, pointing out what they're missing or what I don't like, not to hate them, but to offer suggestions. I also critique my own projects a lot because I want them to do everything.
